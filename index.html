About Us

CoTeX is a textile supply chain and product development company based in Zhejiang, China, with a strong focus on Muslim and traditional apparel markets.

We specialize in hijab fabrics, traditional women’s suit sets (shirt panels, coordinated dupatta, and trouser fabrics), and cotton/rayon/velvet woven textiles designed for Middle Eastern and South Asian consumers.

Over the years, we have built long-term partnerships with dedicated production facilities in China, forming a closely integrated manufacturing network that supports both stability and flexibility.

Unlike traditional trading companies that focus solely on sourcing, CoTeX combines supply chain integration with design-driven development to help partners improve product differentiation and margin structure.

We believe that long-term cooperation is built not only on competitive pricing, but also on stable production, clear communication, and continuous product innovation.
